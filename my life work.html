PART ONE:Local methods of water purification

In our society today the water quality in cameroon is not that the best, but we have many local method in which we can purify our drinking water.

Firstly by boiling

Our water is gotten and put in a boiling pan and heated.This method enables us to kill the germs found in the and ensure it for good drinking and, after which the water is being cooled and keep for drinking.

By filtering with cotten and sand

Water from their various source are gotten and, the water is made to pass through a pile of cotten and sand,the dirt will be held and water pass filtered.Such a method is good form water which contain alot of particles, which cannot be easily removed by the hand

Adding of chlorine liquid

Chlorine is a poisonous substances, which when added to water it kill all the germs,but it should be put in a minute quantity or else it can harm us also.so using a dropper add two to three dros of the liquid chlorine to a twenty litre galon, and finally you saken it and allow it for some time for the bacteria to kill the germs
